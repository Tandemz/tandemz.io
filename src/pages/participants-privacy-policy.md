### 1.Definitions

In this privacy policy, the terms identified below by a capital letter have the following meaning, whether used in the singular or in the plural:

* **Personal Data**: refers to data which, within the meaning of the Regulations applicable to the protection of personal data, make it possible to designate or identify, directly or indirectly, a natural person.
* **Participant:** refers to any natural person of full age and legally capable who accesses the Platform in order to participate in a Session, regardless of their location and the terms of their access.
* **Platform:** refers to the test platform entitled "Tandemz", edited by TANDEMZ and made available to the Participant via the Internet, on the Site.
* **Regulations applicable to the protection of personal data:** refers to any regulations applicable to the protection of personal data and in particular Regulation (EU) No. 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of April 27, 2016 known as "General Data Protection Regulation” or “GDPR” and the amended law n ° 78-17 of 6 January 1978 known as the “Data Protection Act ”.
* **Sessions:** refers to the study and test sessions offered on the Platform, allowing a Participant to participate in a study on a product, a service, a Participant test, a Participant interview, a focus group.
* **Site:** refers to the website [https://www.tandemz.io/](https://www.tandemz.io/ "https://www.tandemz.io/") edited by TANDEMZ and allowing access to the Platform and Sessions.

### 2.**Purposes of the processing of Personal Data**

TANDEMZ processes the Participant's Personal Data for the following purposes:

* Registration and connection of the Participant on the Tandemz Panel Platform;
* Eligibility and participation of the Participant in Sessions:
* Providing support to the Participant during the use of the Platform, as well as maintaining the platform’s continuous use;
* Offering Sessions to Participants according to their profiles; send them the TANDEMZ newsletter if Participanted expressly subscribed to it;
* Improving the performance and functionality of the Platform;
* Prevention and detection of fraud, malware (malicious software) and management of security incidents;
* Management of any disputes.
* Monitoring and analysis of Platform traffic; and
* For statistical purposes.

#### **4. Retention period of Personal Data**

Participants' Personal Data collected for the purposes of registering to the panel, connecting, supporting, improving the performance and functionality of the Platform, preventing fraud, as well as offering the Participant Sessions adapted to his profile are kept for the duration of use of the Platform by the Participant for a period of (.). At the end of this retention period, TANDEMZ will send an information email to the Participant informing them of the upcoming deletion of their Personal Data. The Participant's Personal Data collected in the context of Participants' participation in Sessions are kept for the duration of the Session. Beyond that, Personal Data is anonymized for scientific research and statistical purposes (art. 89, paragraph 1 and 2 of the General Data Protection Regulation of April 27, 2016), the anonymization of this data does not allow identification of the persons concerned.

### **8. Rights of Data Subjects over their Personal Data**

The Data Subjects have, at any time, the following rights over their Personal Data:

* **Right to access:** obtain confirmation of the processing of their Personal Data as well as a certain amount of information on the processing, it being understood that this information is in any event given in this Personal Data protection policy;
* **Right to rectify:** obtain rectification of your Personal Data when they are inaccurate or incomplete;
* **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten"): obtain the erasure of their Personal Data when they are no longer necessary for the purposes for which they were collected or when the Data Subject objects to the processing of his Personal Data;
* **Right to limit processing**: obtain limitation of the processing of their Personal Data when the Data Subject disputes the accuracy of the data, when the data retention period has expired but the Data Subject still needs to keep these data. Personal Data for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims, or if the Data Subject has objected to the processing;
* **Right to portability**: obtain communication of the Personal Data that the Concerned Person has communicated to TANDEMZ in a readable format, or ask TANDEMZ to transmit the Personal Data that the Concerned Person has communicated to another data controller;
* **Right to oppose**: to oppose at any time, for reasons relating to his personal situation, the processing of his Personal Data, in particular in the event that this opposition concerns commercial prospecting, including profiling;
* **Withdrawal of consent**: withdraw consent to the future processing of their Personal Data by TANDEMZ, when the processing is based on consent;
* **Right to file a complaint:** file a complaint with the National Commission for Information Technology and Freedoms if the Data Subject considers that the processing carried out by TANDEMZ constitutes a violation of his Personal Data, online at the address https: / /www.cnil.fr/fr/plaintes or by post to the following address: CNIL - 3 Place de Fontenoy - TSA 80715 - 75334 PARIS CEDEX 07.
